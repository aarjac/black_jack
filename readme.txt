Black Jack Game
###############
-computer dealer
-one human player
-create a normal deck of cards (52)

Steps:
-human player makes a bet
-human player gets two cards face up
-dealer gets two cards one face up
-goal is to get closer to 21 than the dealer without going over
    -hit: receive another card
    -stay: stop receiving cards
-after player goes 
    -if player under 21
        -dealer hits until they beat the player or bust and lose
    -if player busts
        -player loses
-if player wins they get double their bet back

possible outcomes: 
1. Player busts
    -dealer wins
2. Player stops hitting
    -dealer wins
    -dealer busts

-card number equals value
-face cards value 10
ace value 1 or 11
